My name is ChunHong Chan, but feel free to call me Louis! I am studying Computational Media at Georgia Institution of Technology as a sophomore. In the past two years of study abroad in the United States, I knew more about myself and found where my passion is at.

 

Before my study abroad, I was born and raised in Hong Kong, a vibrant and multi-cultural city. My native language is Cantonese, but I am trilingual with English and Mandarin. As a creative person who is enthusiastic in taking on challenges and eager to explore, I am interested in different area.
